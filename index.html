What Makes Our Coffee Special?

Specialty coffee is distinguished from conventional coffee by its careful cultivation, processing, and roasting, as well as its exceptional flavor and traceability. Here are some things that make specialty coffee special:
Flavor
Specialty coffee has complex flavor profiles with fruity, floral, and chocolate notes. It also has a more balanced acidity, which gives it a refreshing nuance.
Density
Specialty coffee beans are denser than non-specialty beans, which means they have more flavor and a longer shelf life.
